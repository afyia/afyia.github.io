Hello!

I’m Afyia, a UX/UI designer based in Orlando, Florida. I’m originally from Brooklyn but I’ve been living in the south a few years now and loving it more every day.


My creative journey started as a kid where my love for art, fashion, and journalism sparked my obsession with color and good typography.


I hold a BA & MA in Communications and New Media and my specialty lies in branding and web design. I’ve spent the past year and a half as a UX/UI Design apprentice learning how to create accessible experiences using human-centered design processes.


Lately I’ve been trying out freelancing and I created this visual identity for local non-profit group, The Autism Gym.


My goal as a designer is to keep learning, growing and pushing the envelope.


I'm always looking for new and exciting work, locally or remote.


Let's create something incredible together!
