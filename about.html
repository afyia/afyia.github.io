Hello I’m Afyia.

I’m a UX/UI designer based in Orlando, Florida. I’m originally from Brooklyn, NY but I’ve been living in the south a few years now and loving it more and more every day.


My creative journey started as a kid where I had a love for art, fashion, and journalism. I would cut images from the NY Times Style section and recreate fashion spreads in my scrapbook. I also loved to draw and thought I could become a Fashion Illustrator. I enrolled in an art school but dropped out after 2 years because the stereotype of a starving artist scared me.


Years later , I still swoon over typogrphy and color and can spend hours happily digging around a website's code.


I earned a BA & MA in Communications and New Media and my specialty lies in branding and web design and development. I've also recently enrolled in a UI/UX Design apprenticeship program where I learn how to crete accessible experiences using human-centered design processes.


I'm an avid reader and when I'm not designing you can find me curled up somewhere with an awesome book.


My goal as a designer is to always be learning, growing and pushing the envelope. I'm always looking for new and exciting work, locally or remote.


Let's create something incredible together.
